Recipes Site Plan

Emily Titus

WDD 131-A3

Overview

Purpose

To provide a place for multiple easy recipes. Often people need help figuring out what to eat and this site will offer them some easy solutions in one convientent spot.

Audience

People that need help figuring out what to make. The main demographic would young adults and mothers.

Dynamic elements

A drop down menu to select which recipe they would like, which will then change what is show to match their selection. As well as marking off the steps as they complete them.
